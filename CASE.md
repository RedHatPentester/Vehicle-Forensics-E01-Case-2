The Scenario 

Michael Appiah, a sales manager at Guba Enterprises Ltd, owns a 2021 Honda Accord Sport (GR
8821-21) insured with Nationwide Insurance Ghana under policy NIG-2021-MV-44821. On 15 March 
2024, he filed a police theft report claiming his vehicle was stolen at 14:00 WAT outside a shop in East 
Legon. He reported that the car was subsequently involved in a collision on Spintex Road at 
approximately 16:28 WAT , while still in the possession of the thief. His insurance agent, Nancy 
Aggrey, filed a claim for GHS 8,300 in vehicle damage just 48 minutes after the police report was 
received. 
Insurance investigator Kofi Mensah flagged the claim and requested a forensic examination of the 
Honda Connect IVI unit. The vehicle was seized and a forensic image was acquired. Your task is to 
determine whether the theft claim is genuine or fraudulent using only data recovered from the IVI 
system.
